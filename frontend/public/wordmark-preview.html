Thesis: "Bhav" comes from भाव (Hindi). Replace the Latin "B" with the actual Devanagari letter भ. No competitor in metal trading does this — it's a cultural moat. The भ has a distinctive top horizontal stroke that visually anchors the wordmark + signals "this is built for India" in 0.2 seconds. The Latin italics for "hav" creates a cross-script tension that's deliberately striking. Bloomberg / IndiaMART / OfBusiness can't do this — it locks in the Indian-trader identity that competitors can't copy.

Thesis: The wordmark is functional, not decorative. A pulsing green dot between "Bhav" and "X" signals MARKETS LIVE in real-time.
