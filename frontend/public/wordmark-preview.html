✦ L2 Variations

L2 · Original (current)

Small bindu, two-layer (gold halo + red-orange core). Subtle, refined.

L2.1 · Sun Core (bigger, glowing radial)

Bindu becomes a full radial sun core — pale center → gold mid → orange outer → deep red rim. Dramatic glow. Pure Surya energy. The light at the center is brighter and more present.

L2.2 · Sun Core + Inner Rays (light escaping between blades)

Same sun core, but with 8 thin gold rays bursting from center toward the gaps between blades. The light isn't contained — it leaks through the spaces. Says: "the divine cannot be held back." More dynamic, more radiant.

L2.3 · Multi-Layer Chakra Bindu

Three concentric layers: outer gold ring + middle dark void + bright sun core. Pure chakra-yantra geometry. Most "sacred geometry" feel — like a Sri Yantra eye.

L2.4 · Living Pulse (animated bindu — markets are alive)

The bindu pulses gently — 2.4-second rhythm, glow expanding and contracting. Says: "the market is alive, BhavX is breathing with it." Functional brand cue. Bloomberg-grade live indicator embedded in the logo.

L2.5 · Sindoor Bindu (pure deep red, sacred mark)

Deeper red, less golden. Pure sindoor/tilak energy — the auspicious mark of devotion. More restrained than L2.1, more sacred than animated. Most refined option for a luxury/Bloomberg positioning.
