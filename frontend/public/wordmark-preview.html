✦ Logo Iterations (camera-iris evolved)

L0 · Current Logo — Reference

The existing 8-parallelogram-blade camera iris. Solid, geometric, recognizable. Iterations below evolve from this baseline.

L1 · Sun-Burst Iris (Surya Halo)

Existing iris + 12 thin radiating rays extending past the blades into a Surya halo. Pairs the camera-iris (precision) with the sun god (radiance). Says: "we capture and we radiate."

L2 · Bindu Center (Sacred Mark)

Existing iris + a tiny sacred bindu at the very center where the blades almost touch. The bindu in Hindu/Tantric tradition is the point of pure consciousness — the still center of the spinning cosmos. Subtle, but loaded. The eye of the iris becomes alive.

L3 · Yantra Outer Ring (Temple Geometry)

Existing iris + a thin outer ring with 8 gentle dots at compass points — yantra/mandala geometry. Adds containment + sacred geometry without crowding. The iris becomes the heart of a ritual diagram.

L4 · Sharper Blades (Aggressive Edge)

Existing iris but with elongated, sharper blades. Less rounded, more dagger-like. The blades feel like they're slicing forward. Same gold gradient. Says: "we cut through chaos."

L5 · Coin Depth (Struck Bullion)

Existing iris + 3D bevel and drop shadow. Looks struck into a gold coin. Each blade has highlight + shadow giving dimension. Says: "this is a piece of currency, not a logo."
